About

Tivey (Lewis Tivey) is a British grunge and alternative rock artist from Burton upon Trent, Staffordshire, UK. The sound lives between melody and abrasion: modern grunge, noise rock, shoegaze textures, and lo-fi distorted guitars. Everything is written, recorded, produced, and released independently.

Related terms: underground grunge, UK grunge, British alternative rock, indie rock, experimental rock, dark alternative, DIY rock, 90s inspired.
