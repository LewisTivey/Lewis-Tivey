Downloads

I’ve put my albums here so you can download them directly.

This is still my work. It’s not public domain. It exists on Spotify, Bandcamp, and the usual machines. If you support it there, that helps. If you don’t, the world will keep turning.

But, I don’t want the music locked behind paywalls or algorithms that decide whether it deserves to exist. Some things are worth more than metrics.

Words are limited. They break down pretty quickly once you get past small talk. Music doesn’t have that problem. It carries the stuff you can’t explain without sounding stupid or dramatic. Call it empathy. Call it something spiritual. Call it noise that happens to mean something. Same thing.

When I was younger, certain bands reached me when people couldn’t. They didn’t save me. They didn’t give answers. They just sat there with me while things were rough. That was enough.

That’s all this is trying to do.

If one of these records finds you at the right time, take it. Sit with it. If you want to support the work, you can donate or buy the albums elsewhere. If you can’t, that’s fine. No guilt, no speech.

I just want the music to survive.
And to end up where it’s supposed to.

— Lewis Tivey
